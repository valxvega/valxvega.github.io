いらっしゃいませ Yokohama!

Places to Visit in Yokohama

Sankeien Garden

A beautiful traditional Japanese garden with historic buildings and seasonal blooms.

Cup Noodles Museum

An interactive museum showcasing the history of instant noodles.

Yokohama Landmark Tower

Enjoy panoramic views of the city and bay from the observation deck.

Red Brick Warehouse

A historic complex with shopping, dining, and event spaces.

Local Food in Yokohama

Shumai Dumplings

A delicious local dumpling, particularly from Kiyoken.

Ramen

Famous for its rich, creamy tonkotsu ramen and soy-based shoyu ramen.

Seafood

Fresh fish from the local markets served in sushi and sashimi.

Kurumicrepe

A sweet, nutty crepe made with walnuts, famous in Yokohama.
